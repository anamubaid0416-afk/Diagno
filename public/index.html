YourMessiah

YourMessiah is an independent information tool. It organises data that diagnostic labs publish themselves — prices, turnaround times, accreditations — alongside averages of public patient reviews. YourMessiah does not rank, rate, or judge labs, and does not originate any claim about them. It is not a medical service and is not a substitute for a doctor. All prices change — confirm directly with the lab before visiting. No lab pays YourMessiah, and YourMessiah earns nothing from any comparison shown.

How YourMessiah works

Where every number comes from

Prices, turnaround times & hours

Taken directly from each lab's official app, official website, or published rate list. The date each price was checked is shown next to it. YourMessiah does not set or estimate prices — it shows what the lab itself publishes.

Accreditations

Verified against public registries — PNAC, the College of American Pathologists directory, and JCI's public accreditation list. YourMessiah only shows an accreditation if the registry confirms it.

Patient-experience figures

These are averages of publicly posted patient reviews — Google reviews and public health forums. They reflect what patients said, aggregated and counted. They are not YourMessiah's assessment. The number of reviews behind each average is always shown.

What YourMessiah does NOT do

YourMessiah does not assign its own score, grade, or rank to any lab. It does not decide which lab is "best." It sorts public data by the filter you choose, and shows you the result. The decision is always yours.

Corrections

Tell us what's wrong

If any price, turnaround time, accreditation, or other figure on YourMessiah is inaccurate or out of date — including if you represent a lab — tell us and we will review and correct it promptly. Accuracy is the whole point of this platform.

corrections@yourmessiah.pk

What to include

The lab and branch, the figure you believe is wrong, the correct figure, and a source if you have one (a screenshot of the lab's app or rate list is ideal). We log every correction and the date it was made.
